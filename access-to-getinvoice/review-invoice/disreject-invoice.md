# Unrejected Invoice

The list of e-invoicing on “Rejected” status can change to “Unreject” by selecting each item and then clicking on “Unreject” button.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (19).png" alt=""><figcaption><p><em>Review Invoice - Unreject</em></p></figcaption></figure>

The confirmation screen will display the list of item for you to reconfirm by clicking on "Unreject” or "Cancel” button.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (53).png" alt=""><figcaption><p><em>Review Invoice - confirm to Unreject</em></p></figcaption></figure>
